Oxjam Shoreditch Takeover 2016

Oxjam Shoreditch Takeover 2016 is part of a national live music festival taking place in over 40 towns and cities across the UK to raise money for Oxfam. Featuring the very best of local music talent, this year, Oxjam Shoreditch will take place in October.
